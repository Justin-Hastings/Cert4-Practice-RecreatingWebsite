WELCOME TO BILLS MARINE – CAIRNS BOATING SPECIALIST

Bills Marine has been established for over 40 years and has become one of the Tropical North’s leading boating, chandlery, marine electronics, accessories and parts and service dealerships in the area. We have based our success on only selling quality products and backing these products with professional advice and factory-trained after sale service.

All of our staff are mad keen boating enthusiasts, whether for fishing, recreation or even commercial purposes. Because of this our staff want to learn more about the industry and therefore are happy to impart their knowledge onto you – our valued customers.
